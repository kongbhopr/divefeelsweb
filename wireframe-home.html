📋 Design Notes & Open Questions

Hero Messaging

H1 must include "กรุงเทพ" and "พัทยา" for SEO. Current draft: "เรียนดำน้ำในกรุงเทพ จบ cert ที่พัทยา ไม่ต้องออกเกาะ". Confirm with owner before hi-fi.

Hero Photo

Need a strong, warm, sunlit underwater photo for the hero. Confirm if owner has this from existing students. If not, stock photo or photoshoot needed (Risk R2).

Course Pricing

Course cards show no prices in wireframe. Open question: will pricing be shown on the home page cards, or only on individual course pages? Recommend: show on course pages only.

Google Business Profile

Reviews widget requires verified GBP. This should be started NOW (dependency D2 — can take 1–2 weeks). Also critical for local SEO "คอร์สดำน้ำ กรุงเทพ" keyword.

Language — TH or TH+EN?

Wireframe uses Thai-first copy. Decision 2.6 (language strategy) must be confirmed before hi-fi. If bilingual: adds ~30–50% dev effort for proper i18n + hreflang.

Divemaster / Pro Track

Persona 4 (Future DM) not prominently featured in current home layout. Options: (a) add DM card to course grid, (b) add a separate "Pro Track" section below courses. Confirm with owner.
